About Pandas

Giant pandas (often referred to as simply “pandas”) are black and
white bears. In the wild, they are found in thick bamboo forests,
high up in the mountains of central China – you can check out our
cool facts about China, here!
These magnificent mammals are omnivores. But whilst they will
occasionally eat small animals and fish, bamboo counts for 99 percent
of their diet.
